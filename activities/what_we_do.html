What we do

At TYA we know that our services can make a difference and this year we will be working with
young people who have suffered abuse so that they can heal from their experiences.

COUNSELLING

Together4Youths is working with trained counselors
and therapists, offering a wide range of support for
young people including Drama therapy
and Art Therapy from ages 5 upwards. Support includes

One on One support

We offer a number of specialist one on one support
courses that deal with social media, anger management,
relationships, sexual exploitation and self-harming.
Through zoom calls and partnered institutions.

Education and Prevention

TYA runs a range of Early Intervention projects
in and out of term time. We want our projects
to transform the lives of young people who attend,
whether that is through finding friends, developing
their self-esteem and confidence, reconsidering risky
behaviour or finally feeling comfortable and safe enough
to speak openly to someone who understands.
These projects are based on important topics which
young people face today such as Bullying, Internet Safety,
Child Sexual Exploitation, Healthy Relationships positive
and negative relationships, drugs and alcohol abuse
and gang culture. etc
