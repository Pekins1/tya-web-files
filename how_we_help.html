ONE ON ONE SUPPORT

We offer a number of specialised one on one support courses that deal with social media, anger management, relationships, sexual exploitation and self-harming.

Remote support

Through zoom calls and other social apps, we offer remote counselling for those who are not able to meet a counselor physically.

Referral to other Agencies

Referrals to other agencies for service not offered by TYA.

EDUCATION AND PREVENTION

TYA runs a range of Early Intervention projects in and out of term time. We want our projects to transform the lives of young people who attend, whether that is through finding friends, developing their self-esteem and confidence, reconsidering risky behaviour or finally feeling comfortable and safe enough to speak openly to someone who understands.


These projects are based on important topics which young people face today such as Bullying, Internet Safety, Child Sexual Exploitation, Healthy Relationships positive and negative relationships, drugs and alcohol abuse and gang culture. etc
