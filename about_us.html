Who we are

ABOUT Together for Youth association

TYA is an independent and non discriminatory charity organisation. For over six (6) years today, Together for Youths A. has been joining hands with youths all over Africa to create impact in our communities. We support and empower everyone affected by or at risk of sexual abuse and rape. We support everyone regardless of Age, Marital Status, Gender Identity, sexual orientation.

our goal

Our goal is to work with hundreds of students in schools, youth clubs and other settings on creative projects that would help them to gain new skills and build confidence as well as learning how to stay safe. We will be training teachers, youth leaders and other professionals in child protection and safeguarding and enable them to understand and work with children and young people.

our mission

Our Mission is To provide specialist, tailored support for anyone affected by or at risk of sexual abuse and rape.
